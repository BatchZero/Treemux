# Third-Party Libraries

Treemux bundles the following open-source packages. Their licenses are reproduced below or linked.

---

## Citadel
**Repository:** https://github.com/orlandos-nl/Citadel  
**License:** MIT  
Used for SFTP/SSH password-auth fallback.

---

## Sparkle
**Repository:** https://github.com/sparkle-project/Sparkle  
**License:** MIT  
Used for over-the-air app updates.

---

## CodeEditSourceEditor
**Repository:** https://github.com/CodeEditApp/CodeEditSourceEditor  
**License:** MIT  
Used for the syntax-highlighting source editor pane.

## CodeEditTextView (BatchZero fork)
**Repository:** https://github.com/BatchZero/CodeEditTextView  
**License:** MIT  
Fork of CodeEditApp/CodeEditTextView that patches a wrapped-line fragment bug.
Original: https://github.com/CodeEditApp/CodeEditTextView

---

## swift-markdown-ui (MarkdownUI)
**Repository:** https://github.com/gonzalezreal/swift-markdown-ui  
**License:** MIT  
Used to render Markdown previews in the file viewer.

## NetworkImage
**Repository:** https://github.com/gonzalezreal/NetworkImage  
**License:** MIT  
Transitive dependency of swift-markdown-ui; renders remote images inside Markdown.

## swift-cmark
**Repository:** https://github.com/apple/swift-cmark  
**License:** BSD 2-Clause "Simplified"  
Transitive dependency of swift-markdown-ui; provides the CommonMark parser (cmark C library).
